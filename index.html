About the Project

TennoTrader analyzes Warframe Market data in real time to surface items worth buying, selling, or flipping based on demand, supply and price trends.

What it does

  • Processes hundreds of items in seconds
  • Ranks best and worst deals via a custom score
  • Generates a Top‑10 and full market view
  • Exports insights and supports local builds
